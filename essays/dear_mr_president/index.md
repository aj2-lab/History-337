# Overview of Political Music

I argue that political music addressed to the president
represents a tradition of political letter writing, and can be used to understand shifting political opinions
and attitudes over time based on the topics of the songs, and how the ideas are presented.

These songs can illustrate issues that people placed
importance onto, and how acceptable these ideas were to the general public.
